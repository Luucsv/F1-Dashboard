Round 1 — Bahrain Grand Prix
The season opener sets the tone
Verstappen overtakes Hamilton on lap 53 at Turn 4 but is ordered to give the position back after exceeding track limits. Hamilton wins, but the fierce rivalry immediately ignites from race one.
Hamilton wins — 25 pts
R1
Round 2 — Emilia Romagna Grand Prix
Verstappen blocks, Hamilton spins
At Tosa corner, Verstappen denies Hamilton passage wheel-to-wheel. Hamilton spins through the gravel and damages his front wing. Verstappen wins and takes the championship lead for the first time.
Verstappen wins — 26 pts
R2
Round 6 — Azerbaijan Grand Prix
Verstappen's tyre blowout costs the lead
Leading with 5 laps remaining at over 300 km/h on the pit straight, Verstappen suffers a catastrophic rear tyre blowout. Pérez wins for Red Bull. Hamilton misses a chance to score heavily after braking too late at the restart.
Pérez wins — Verstappen DNF
R6
Round 10 — British Grand Prix
Hamilton crashes Verstappen on lap 1
At the high-speed Copse corner on lap 1, the two cars make contact. Verstappen barrel-rolls into the barrier at enormous speed and is taken to hospital. Hamilton gets a 10-second penalty but still wins to the delight of the home crowd.
Hamilton wins — Verstappen hospitalized
R10
Round 14 — Italian Grand Prix
Both title rivals crash out at Monza
After Hamilton pits ahead of Verstappen, both fight aggressively into the chicane. Verstappen's car mounts over Hamilton's Mercedes with the Red Bull tyre resting on Hamilton's helmet — saved only by the halo. Both retire. Ricciardo wins for McLaren.
Ricciardo wins — Both DNF
R14
Round 19 — São Paulo Grand Prix
Hamilton's greatest comeback drive
Starting 10th after a grid penalty, Hamilton battles through the entire field. He passes Verstappen around the outside at Turn 4 in an iconic overtake and wins by over 10 seconds — widely considered the drive of the decade.
Hamilton wins from P10
R19
Round 21 — Saudi Arabian Grand Prix
Controversy in Jeddah — level on points
In a chaotic race with two red flags, Verstappen deliberately slows causing Hamilton to rear-end him. Despite a penalty for Verstappen, Hamilton wins. Going into the finale both drivers are tied on points — the first time since 1974.
Hamilton wins — tied on points
R21
Round 22 — Abu Dhabi Grand Prix
Verstappen wins the title on the final lap
In the most controversial finish in modern F1, a late safety car allows only the lapped cars between the two title rivals to unlap themselves. Verstappen, on fresh soft tyres, overtakes Hamilton on the very last lap to win his first World Championship by just 8 points.
Verstappen — 2021 World Champion
